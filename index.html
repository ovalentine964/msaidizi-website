It started with one question: Why does my mum's business have no data?

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

Is Msaidizi really free?

+

Yes. Msaidizi is free forever for workers. No hidden charges, no premium version, no ads. We believe the people who need it most shouldn't have to pay for it.

What phone do I need?

+

Any Android phone with 2GB+ RAM running Android 6.0 or higher. Samsung A15, Tecno Spark, Xiaomi Redmi, Nokia — the phones most Kenyans already own. Msaidizi is only 15MB.

Is my data safe?

+

Your data stays on your phone. Voice recordings are processed on-device and deleted. No one sees your business unless you choose to share. We comply with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019.

Do I need internet?

+

No. Msaidizi works 100% offline. The AI runs on your phone. You only need internet if you want to back up your data (optional).

How is this different from M-Shwari or Tala?

+

M-Shwari and Tala lend money. They don't help you run your business. Msaidizi tracks your sales, expenses, profits, and debts. It helps you understand your business first — then (someday) connects you to credit based on real data, not just your M-Pesa history.

Who is behind Msaidizi?

+

Biashara Intelligence was founded by Valentine Owuor, a BSc Economics & Statistics graduate from Migori. We're based in Migori, Kenya — not Nairobi, not Silicon Valley. We build for the market, from the market.
