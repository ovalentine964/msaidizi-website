About Us

Making Africa's Invisible Economy Visible

Africa's informal economy employs over 600 million people and generates an estimated $1.3 trillion in annual output. Yet it remains virtually invisible to policymakers, financial institutions, and development organizations.

Biashara Intelligence exists to change that. We built Msaidizi — a voice-based, offline-first app — to help workers track their money. The data becomes intelligence that transforms how banks lend, how governments plan, and how businesses distribute.

Our Mission

Provide economic intelligence to Africa's 600M+ informal workers by making invisible workers visible through data, fixing market inefficiencies, information asymmetry, and coordination failures.

How It Works

From Workers to Intelligence

Three simple steps transform everyday economic activity into intelligence that changes lives.

01
🎤

Workers Speak

Mama Mboga tells Msaidizi what she sold today — in her own language, with her own voice. No typing. No literacy needed.

02
🧠

AI Transforms

Millions of voice notes become structured economic data — demand patterns, income flows, market dynamics. Privacy-preserved, aggregated intelligence.

03
📊

Stakeholders Act

Banks lend smarter. Governments plan better. Businesses distribute efficiently. Workers get credit. Everyone wins.

Installation Guide

How to Install Msaidizi

Follow these simple steps to get Msaidizi running on your Android device.

1

Download Msaidizi

Tap the download button. The app downloads automatically — everything is included, no extra files needed.

2

Tap Install

When the download finishes, Android will ask: "Do you want to install this app?" Just tap Install. That's it — no Settings, no extra steps.

3

Start Using
