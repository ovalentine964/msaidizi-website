About Us

Making Africa's Invisible Economy Visible

Africa's informal economy employs over 600 million people and generates an estimated $1.3 trillion in annual output. Yet it remains virtually invisible to policymakers, financial institutions, and development organizations.

Biashara Intelligence exists to change that. We built Msaidizi — a voice-based, offline-first app — to help workers track their money. The data becomes intelligence that transforms how banks lend, how governments plan, and how businesses distribute.

Privacy First

Your Data Never Leaves Your Phone

We use federated learning to improve our AI without ever seeing your data. Your transactions, your sales, your numbers — they stay on your device.

01
🔐

Federated Learning

Your phone trains a small AI model locally. Only anonymous model improvements — never your actual data — are sent to our server.

02
🛡️

Differential Privacy

Mathematical guarantee (ε=0.1) that no one can reverse-engineer your data from the aggregated model. Used by Apple and Google.

03
👥

K-Anonymity

Your data is only used when at least 5 other workers have similar patterns. You're always hidden in a crowd — impossible to identify individually.

04
🏛️

Data Sovereignty

African data processed in Africa, by African infrastructure. Compliant with Kenya DPA, Nigeria NDPR, and South Africa POPIA.
