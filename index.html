Our Mission

Started with one question: Why does my mum's business have no data?

She sells mandazi in Gikomba. She works harder than any CEO. But no bank sees her. No government counts her. No company studies her market.

Biashara AI changes that. We're building the data infrastructure for Africa's informal economy — starting with the workers themselves.
