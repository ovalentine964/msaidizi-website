About Us

Making Africa's Invisible Economy Visible

Africa's informal economy employs over 200 million people and generates an estimated $1.3 trillion in annual output. Yet it remains virtually invisible to policymakers, financial institutions, and development organizations.

Biashara Intelligence exists to change that. We collect real-time economic data from the ground up — through the workers and businesses themselves — and transform it into actionable intelligence.

Founded by Valentine Owuor

BSc Economics & Statistics · Migori, Kenya

Our Mission

To build the data infrastructure that Africa's informal economy deserves — enabling better policy, smarter finance, and inclusive growth for 200 million workers.

What We Do

Our three-pillar approach transforms raw economic activity into intelligence that drives decisions.

Meet Msaidizi

Msaidizi ('Helper' in Swahili) is our voice-based mobile application designed for Africa's informal workers. It captures economic activity through simple voice notes — no typing, no literacy barriers, no internet required.

  • Voice-first: speak in your language
  • Offline-first: works without internet
  • Privacy-first: your data, your control
  • Built in Migori, for Migori, for Africa

Installation Guide

How to Install Msaidizi

Follow these simple steps to get Msaidizi running on your Android device.

1

Allow Unknown Sources

Go to Settings → Security → enable 'Unknown Sources' (or Settings → Apps → Special access on newer Android). This allows you to install apps outside the Play Store.

2

Download the APK

Tap the download button above or visit the GitHub Releases page. The APK file will download to your device.

3

Open and Install

Open the downloaded APK file from your notifications or Downloads folder. Tap 'Install' when prompted.

4

First Launch

On first launch, Msaidizi will download voice models (~80MB). This requires an internet connection. After that, the app works fully offline.
